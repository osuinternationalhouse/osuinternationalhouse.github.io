About Property

The International House is a great place for international students to live and offers several advantages while attending Oregon State University. This relaxed and quiet atmosphere is great for studying. Our fully-furnished rooms make it easy to move in. The house location is close to both the Oregon State University campus and downtown Corvallis. You won’t find a more affordable and convenient residence while attending college.

Rates / Amenities / Apply

Rent

  • $475-$525 / month (depending on choice and availability of room)
  • First and last month’s rent are due prior to move in.
  • $150 cleaning/security deposit ($100 is refundable).

Free Utilities

  • Wireless Internet
  • Cable TV
  • Electricity
  • Water and Sewer
  • Garbage Service
  • On-Premise Laundry

Room Features

  • Single Occupancy Room
  • Bed & Bedding
  • Closet
  • Desk, Chair and Table
  • Refrigerator
  • Digital Door Lock
  • shared bathroom with up to 3 tenants.

Other Benefits

  • Use of a large common kitchen in which each tenant is assigned upper and lower cabinets and countertop.
  • dishes and appliances.
  • Common dining, living, basement storage, and outdoor areas.
  • Close to campus and downtown.
  • Free street parking
  • Bike Rack
  • Piano
